When Weave is the right call

You want one login for everything. Weave combines payments, scheduling, reviews, reactivation, phone. If that's your evaluation criteria, Weave wins by design.

You need 40+ PMS integrations. Weave has them live. We have Open Dental live, 4 more behind partner-program activation.

When usdpr. is the right call

You already have a phone system you like. Paying $500–900/month for Weave when you only need reactivation is over-buying. $297–$697/month with us does the one job better.

You want to be live this week, not next month. Weave onboarding is 5–14 days with an implementation specialist. Ours is 15 minutes self-serve. No call, no demo required, no account manager wait.

You want to see the reactivation numbers specifically. Weave shows aggregate "patient engagement." We ship a cohort-analytics dashboard with reply rate × book rate × segment × day-of-week — the actual levers to turn.

Transparent pricing matters. Weave's $249 "starting price" becomes $500–$900 bundled with phones after the sales call. Our $297 / $697 / $1,997 are published with the patient caps. Same number every month.
