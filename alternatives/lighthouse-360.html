Four honest reasons practices leave Lighthouse 360 for usdpr.

Reactivation lives in the broadcast-blast paradigm

Schedule a mass send, watch the open rate. usdpr. runs a per-patient, sequenced approach tuned to segment (A1/A2 broken-appointment vs. B1/B2/B3 overdue-hygiene).

1-year contract norm

Lighthouse typically signs annual. usdpr. is month-to-month with one-click cancel from the dashboard. Data export under 1 hour, any time.

Henry Schein One ecosystem lock-in

Lighthouse pairs tightly with Dentrix/Dentrix Enterprise and Henry Schein payments. Best-of-breed buyers get pulled into the suite.

Reply-intent is manual

Lighthouse surfaces incoming SMS. It doesn't classify (interested / question / objection / unsubscribe) or draft a response. usdpr. does both.
