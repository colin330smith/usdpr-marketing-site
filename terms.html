§ 01

What this document does.

This Terms of Service ("Terms") is the contract between USDPR LLC ("USDPR," "we," "us") and the orthodontic practice or multi-location group that subscribes to our software ("Customer," "you"). By signing a pilot agreement, an order form, clicking "I agree," or by accessing the product, you enter into this contract.

Three documents govern the relationship, in order of precedence: (1) a signed Business Associate Agreement, (2) a signed order form or pilot agreement, and (3) these Terms. Where any conflict exists between (1) and these Terms, the BAA controls.

§ 02

Who can sign this.

You must be an authorized signatory of the orthodontic practice or the corporate entity that holds the practice's operating license. You represent that you have authority to bind that entity. If you're signing on behalf of a DSO, you represent that you have authority to bind each affiliated practice to be onboarded.

§ 03

Subscription, Recovery Launch, and billing.

The Recovery Launch fee

A one-time Recovery Launch fee of $1,500 is paid at order. It covers: BAA execution, PMS integration, the 72-hour Recovery Opportunity audit, the first ranked call list, and the OIG Advisory Opinion 25-08 fair-market-value memo filed to your record. The Recovery Launch fee is fully refundable under §04 if the 60-day shown-value guarantee is not met.

Monthly subscription — Ortho Recovery Pro

Monthly subscription is $997 per practice, per calendar month, billed in advance on the 1st of each month. Billing begins on the first day of the calendar month following delivery of the 72-hour audit.

Founding cohort pricing. The first ten (10) orthodontic practices to sign a pilot agreement are locked into a founding-cohort monthly rate of $697/month for as long as the subscription remains active without a break exceeding 60 days. This rate does not reset on renewal and does not apply to the Scale tier.

Multi-location — Ortho Recovery Scale

Practices with 2 or more orthodontic locations are billed at $1,497 per location, per month under the Scale tier. Minimum commitment for Scale is six (6) months; after the initial six months, the subscription is month-to-month. DSOs with 10+ locations may negotiate a custom master subscription agreement.

Payment terms

  • All fees are in US Dollars and are exclusive of taxes.
  • Payment method: ACH, wire, or major credit card via Stripe.
  • Invoices are due net 0 (pre-paid monthly) unless we've agreed otherwise in writing.
  • Past-due amounts accrue interest at the lesser of 1.0% per month or the maximum permitted by Florida law.

Price changes

We may change monthly subscription pricing with 60 days' written notice. Founding-cohort rates are excluded from price changes per the clause above. A price change gives you the right to terminate for convenience at the end of your current billing period with no penalty.

§ 04

The $5,000 shown-value guarantee.

Operating Principle § 16 · Sealed

$5,000 of attributed shown-patient value in 60 days. Or your Launch Fee is refunded.

Advertised in accordance with 16 CFR Part 239. Refunded via ACH within 10 business days of eligibility determination. No debate, no hoops, no retention negotiation.

How it's measured

"Shown-patient value" means the aggregate fee-schedule value of patients who (a) were identified by USDPR in one of the eight Recovery Opportunity states during the 60-day window, and (b) physically arrived at your practice for an appointment during the same window, measured net of a matched hold-out control group inside your own chart that never received recovery outreach. In plain terms: we subtract out the visits that would have shown up anyway.

Eligibility conditions

  • BAA and order form executed by both parties.
  • PMS connection established within 10 business days of contract signature.
  • Customer provides a designated staff member to receive and act on the weekly Friday call list.
  • Customer acts on at least 60% of the weekly Recovery Opportunities surfaced (logged through the product).
  • 60-day window runs from the date of first signed audit delivery.

What we refund

If eligibility is met and the attributed shown-value is less than $5,000, we refund the full $1,500 Recovery Launch fee in the original form of payment within 10 business days. Monthly subscription fees already paid are refunded prorated from the eligibility-determination date through the end of the paid billing period. You may terminate the subscription at that point with no further fee.

What we don't refund

  • SMS pass-through costs billed by Twilio (if any).
  • Recovery Launch fees where eligibility conditions were not met.
  • Third-party services (CareCredit fees, Stripe transaction fees, etc.).

The guarantee does not create an exclusive remedy: it sits alongside whatever other rights you have under these Terms or applicable law.

BAA precedence.

Where PHI is involved — which is the majority of the working relationship — the signed BAA between your practice and USDPR controls over these Terms. In any conflict, the BAA wins. Where the BAA is silent, these Terms fill in.

We will not begin processing PHI until the BAA is executed by both parties. Customers demanding an alternative BAA template should expect a good-faith negotiation; we do not sign template BAAs that would make us liable for HHS fines beyond the scope of our actual acts.
