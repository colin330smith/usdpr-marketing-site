usdpr. Why 6 practices max
A real constraint, not a marketing flourish

This isn't agency work. By design.

At six active practices, I can still personally read every patient reply, write every Friday report, and know every quirk of every dentist's list. Past that number, the work degrades into "agency work" — account managers, pooled writers, lost context.

If we fill up, there's a waitlist. I'd rather be out of capacity than compromise the product.

Why only 612 / 15
usdpr. Honest terms
What's on paper

I don't guarantee results. I guarantee the work.

What I guarantee
The outreach happens. The report ships Friday. The BAA is in place before any patient data moves. Cancellation is clean, 7 days.
What I don't
I can't guarantee X patients rebooked or $Y recovered. Reactivation is probabilistic — depends on list quality, consent, seasonality, your front-desk follow-up.
What happens if it flops
By week 3 of the pilot, if the report isn't showing movement, I tell you to cancel before you ask. I'd rather lose a pilot than earn a bad reputation.
