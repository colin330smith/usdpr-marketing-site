Methodology

All outreach ran on behalf of [PRACTICE NAME], under the practice's name and phone number. usdpr. operates as a HIPAA Business Associate under signed BAA; every send, reply, and booking was logged with patient ID, campaign ID, and timestamp in an encrypted audit trail. SMS rate-limited to 1 per day, 3 per week per patient, per TCPA best practice. STOP replies honored instantly via DNC list.

Per-visit production figures sourced from the practice's own ledger, post-appointment. "Shown + restorative" entries indicate patients whose hygiene visit surfaced additional treatment accepted during or within 14 days of the appointment — this downstream production is real but excluded from the pilot ROI calculation to keep the numbers conservative.
