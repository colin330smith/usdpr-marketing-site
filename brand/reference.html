§ 03 STANDING SECTIONS · TAXONOMY Eight standing columns

Eight standing sections. Every chart writes them.

The journal covers eight recurring states that every orthodontic chart generates. Each has its own column, query, script, and attribution window. These are not marketing categories; they are SQL predicates against your practice management system.

§ 03.1 · 01 RET18% of debond chart

Retainer Check Overdue

Post-debond patient missed their scheduled retainer review. Relapse clock running from the moment the appliance came off.

§ 03.2 · 02 OBS24% of obs chart

Observation Overdue

Pre-treatment child on observation recall. Growth window closing; drift becomes a competitor consult in 18 months.

§ 03.3 · 03 CON31% of consults

Consult No-Start

Consulted, quoted, did not proceed. A scripted re-touch at 21 and 60 days recaptures a measurable fraction.

§ 03.4 · 04 NSH11% weekly

No-Show, Not Rescheduled

Missed appointment that never got re-booked. The PMS reports the miss; it does not close the loop on your behalf.

§ 03.5 · 05 DFM22% of debonds

Debond Follow-Up Missed

Post-treatment patient dropped out of the follow-up sequence. Chart says check in at month-3 and month-6.

§ 03.6 · 06 FGC19% of approvals

Financing Gone Cold

Approved for CareCredit or equivalent, never started treatment. Dedicated 14-day and 45-day recovery scripts.

§ 03.7 · 07 IFS14% of families

Inactive Family / Siblings

Current patient has a sibling of treatment age with no consult scheduled. The highest-converting state in the set.

§ 03.8 · 08 FPRopen

Former Patient Return

Completed treatment 18–60 months ago. Candidate for refinement, new retainers, aligner touch-up, or teen-sibling consult.
