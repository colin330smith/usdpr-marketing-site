Eight states. Every chart has them.

USDPR models the ortho recovery pipeline as eight discrete patient states — each with its own trigger, script, and shown-up attribution. Not a generic reactivation funnel. Not bulk SMS. Eight distinct queries against your PMS.

# Code State Trigger Typical ratio
