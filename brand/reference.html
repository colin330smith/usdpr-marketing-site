§ 03 TAXONOMY

Eight states. Every chart has them.

The recovery pipeline modeled as eight discrete patient states. Each with its own trigger, script, and attribution. Not a generic reactivation funnel; eight specific queries against your PMS.

§ 03.1 · 01 RET 18% of debond chart

Retainer Check Overdue

Post-debond patient missed their scheduled retainer review. Relapse clock running from the moment the appliance came off.

§ 03.2 · 02 OBS 24% of obs chart

Observation Overdue

Pre-treatment child on observation recall. Growth window closing; every month of drift is a competitor consult in 18 months.

§ 03.3 · 03 CON 31% of consults

Consult No-Start

Consulted, quoted, did not proceed. A scripted re-touch at 21 and 60 days recaptures a measurable portion of them.

§ 03.4 · 04 NSH 11% weekly

No-Show, Not Rescheduled

Missed appointment that never got re-booked. PMS reports the miss; it does not close the loop on your behalf.

§ 03.5 · 05 DFM 22% of debonds

Debond Follow-Up Missed

Post-treatment patient dropped out of the follow-up sequence. Chart says you should check in at month-3 and month-6.

§ 03.6 · 06 FGC 19% of approvals

Financing Gone Cold

Approved for CareCredit or equivalent, never started. Dedicated 14- and 45-day re-touch scripts, one per product.

§ 03.7 · 07 IFS 14% of families

Inactive Family / Siblings

Current patient has a sibling of treatment age with no consult scheduled. Highest-converting state in the entire set.

§ 03.8 · 08 FPR open

Former Patient Return

Completed treatment 18–60 months ago. Candidate for refinement, new retainers, aligner touch-up, or teen-sibling consult.
