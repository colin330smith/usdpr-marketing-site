NewThe 30-in-60 outcome guarantee

  • $5,000 attributed shown-value in 60 days. Or Launch Fee refunded. No credit card up front. We don't charge until we've delivered. If we miss, billing never starts and you keep every patient we brought back along the way. Practice + Practice Pro tiers, first 10 practices this quarter. Pilot details.

  • /for-orthodontics landing — our specialty wedge at its sharpest. Six treatment-phase sequences (retainer-check 6/12/18mo, paused-treatment, debond follow-up, consult-to-schedule, referring-GP loop).
  • Dentrix CSV adapter — ships alongside the existing Open Dental adapter. 67 passing unit tests. Covers G5-G7 variants, 7 date formats, minor filtering, DNC, unicode, delimiter sniffing.
  • Email funnel updated — all 3 nurture touches + audit-delivery email lead with the guarantee. Subject lines: "guarantee slot closing" replaces "14-day trial."
  • Footer reorganized — "The guarantee" is the first footer item. New "Switching from" column. "Compliance & legal" column.
