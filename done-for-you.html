Is this you?

When Done-for-you makes sense — and when it doesn't.

Say yes if

These describe your practice.

  • Zero front-desk lift is non-negotiable — you want outreach happening without your team approving queues.
  • Your brand voice matters. You want templates written in your tone, not template-library defaults, tuned monthly.
  • You've tried reactivation tools before and bounced — because the work still landed on the office manager.
  • You want a strategic partner, not just software. Monthly campaign reviews. Segmentation tuning. Channel-mix recommendations.
  • You're on Practice Pro or DSO scale — this tier earns back its cost at ~3,000+ active patients.
  • You're an ortho / prostho / OMFS practice where specialty-aware campaign writing is table stakes.
Say no if

These describe your needs.

  • Your patient count is under 1,500. The self-serve Practice tier ($997/mo) is the right fit.
  • You enjoy the control of approving replies yourself. Self-serve keeps you in the loop on every send.
  • You're shopping for a $50/mo tool. DFY is not cheap. If budget is the constraint, self-serve is still great.
  • You want a general marketing agency — Google Ads, SEO, new-patient acquisition. We don't do those. We do reactivation, deep.
  • You want per-patient pricing. Not legal. Not building it. See compliance.
What's included

Six concierge services on every DFY tenant.

01Onboarding
White-glove setup, live
Three live sessions with Colin: PMS mapping, template voice calibration, cohort segmentation. Your list is launch-ready at the end of week one, without a single form filled by you.
02Template authoring
Written in your voice
Founder-written email + SMS + phone scripts tuned to your practice's voice. Review and green-light once; they ship. Re-tuned monthly based on reply data.
03Reply triage
Founder-reviewed, pre-send
Every AI-drafted reply gets a human glance before it leaves. Nothing weird goes out. Anything tricky is escalated to your office manager with a recommended action.
04Weekly tuning
Segmentation + cadence
Weekly review of reply + book rates by segment and channel. Slow cohorts get re-engaged, weak templates get rewritten, cadence windows get tightened. Live, hands-on.
05Monthly strategy call
30-min, founder-direct
A 30-minute call where Colin walks you through last month's numbers — what worked, what didn't, what to change. Not a CSM. Not a ticket. The person building the product.
06Quarterly benchmark
Your numbers vs. peers
Anonymized, opt-in benchmarking against other DFY practices of similar size and specialty. See whether your reactivation rate is top-decile or has room. Data stays walled between tenants.
