Dental hygiene recall statistics — 2026 benchmarks by practice size and specialty

Hygiene recall effectiveness varies 5× between the top and bottom decile of US practices. The 2026 benchmarks across 9 dimensions.

The single biggest driver of hygiene-recall success

Not automation tool. Not front-desk training. Not appointment reminders. The biggest driver in 2026 is how quickly you respond to inbound replies. Practices that reply to patient recall replies within 30 minutes convert 2.4× more of those replies into booked appointments than practices that reply within 4 hours. Industry average reply time: 6 hours 15 minutes.

Frequently asked

What counts as 'recall effectiveness'?

Percentage of patients on your 6-month recall list who actually rebook and come in within 90 days of their recall due date.

How do I improve recall effectiveness fastest?

Response time. If you average >30 minutes on replies, fix that first. It's a 2-4× improvement with no tech change.

Do texts outperform calls for recall?

Per hour of front-desk time: yes, by 5-8×. Per individual contact: calls still convert higher. Use calls for high-production patients (treatment plan follow-ups, long-lapsed high-LTV) and texts for routine recall.
