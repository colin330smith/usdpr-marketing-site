The 5 segments of lapsed dental patients — and how to write for each.

6 min read · 2026-04-19

A patient who broke their appointment last Tuesday and a patient who hasn't been seen since 2023 are not the same customer. They need different messages, different urgency, different proof. If you write one "we miss you!" template and send it to both, you get the reply rate of the weaker cohort (5%) instead of the stronger (18%).

Here's the 5-segment framework usdpr. uses — built from years of industry benchmark data — and the template shape that works for each.

Why one-size-fits-all fails

Dental practices that send one "we miss you" message to every lapsed patient hit ~7% blended reply rate. Practices that segment and write per-cohort hit 11–13% blended. On 500 contactable patients that's the difference between 35 replies and 60 — and 60 replies turns into roughly 10 more booked appointments per cycle.
