Will my front desk actually use this, or will it sit unused like the last tool?
They don't touch it daily. usdpr. runs the outreach on autopilot. The only time your front desk engages is when a patient books or asks a clarifying question \u2014 and when they do, it arrives in their inbox with pre-drafted replies they can approve in one click. No new dashboard to check. No training webinar. If they can manage the existing recall list, they can manage this.
My PMS is Dentrix / Eaglesoft / [something not Open Dental]. Doesn't the sync have to be native?
No \u2014 CSV import works on day one. Open Dental has a direct REST sync live today. Dentrix, Eaglesoft, and Curve are scaffolded and activate in 1\u20134 weeks. Until then, you export a CSV from your PMS once a week (we provide the exact export instructions for every major system) and upload it \u2014 auto column-detect handles the rest. Same analytics, same reactivation rates.
What about HIPAA? I'm not sending patient data to a new vendor without looking at this carefully.
Right instinct. We sign a full HIPAA Business Associate Agreement via HelloSign before any patient data moves. PHI scrubber enforced at the database query layer \u2014 no treatment names, diagnoses, or procedure codes ever reach a message body. SOC 2 Type I complete; Type II in audit period. Counsel-reviewed before any client signs.
Isn't this what Weave / RevenueWell / Solutionreach already does? I pay them $400/mo already.
Yes and no. They bundle reactivation with 20 other features \u2014 and the reactivation piece is usually their weakest. We do one thing, deep. Reactivation-specific segmentation (A1/A2 broken-appointment, B1/B2/B3 overdue-hygiene). Reply-intent classification. Cross-channel cohort analytics. You can run usdpr. alongside your existing tool for the trial \u2014 side-by-side on the same list \u2014 and see the reactivation rate difference directly.
What if it doesn't work for my practice? I've been burned by dental SaaS before.
One-click cancel from the dashboard. No retention call, no cancellation fee, no "contact support to cancel" flow. Data export in under an hour \u2014 you take every patient, every reply, every booking with you as CSV + JSON. Pilot-program practices get a 30-day money-back guarantee: fewer than 10 rebookings in month 1, we refund that month within 7 days. See the pilot page.
I'm a solo dentist \u2014 who's actually supporting this if I have a problem?
Me. Colin. Personally. No tier-1 help desk. Email me, reply within 2 business hours or that day's fee comes off your invoice. I read every onboarding. I answer every question. Read the founder letter if you want the long version of the accountability story.
