02
Legal & counsel

Reviewed by Florida healthcare counsel. Before we ever touch patient data.

Dental reactivation sits at the intersection of HIPAA, TCPA, CAN-SPAM, state anti-kickback statutes, and state dental-board rules. Getting any one wrong is a felony or a license issue. So the entire product — terms, BAA template, pricing model, message templates — is reviewed by a Florida healthcare attorney before it touches a practice's data.

HIPAA BAA HelloSign e-signed before any PHI moves. Covers workforce, subprocessors, breach notification, and return/destroy on termination. Counsel-reviewed 2026-Q1
TCPA A2P 10DLC Carrier-registered campaigns with enforced rate limits (1/day, 3/week per patient). STOP honored immediately and permanently. Twilio brand verified
CAN-SPAM Physical-address footer, functional unsubscribe, honest subject lines, 10-business-day removal SLA — codified in the send pipeline. Enforced pre-send
FL §817.505 Flat SaaS fee only. Florida §817.505 (Patient Brokering Act) makes per-patient referral fees a felony. Our pricing is designed to sit cleanly inside the referral-services safe harbor. Never per-rebook
State dental boards FL Rule 64B5-4.002 exempts a dentist's agent from solicitation rules when contacting existing or former patients. We only contact your patients, under your practice name. Existing-patient only

Per-orthodontic patient recovery fees are a felony in Florida under §817.505. The flat-fee model isn't a marketing choice — it's the only compliant structure.

03
Security posture

Engineered to the HIPAA Security Rule, then independently audited.

Every send, reply, and booking is logged with an immutable audit trail. Data at rest on encrypted volumes (AES-256). Data in transit is TLS 1.2+. Role-based access with least-privilege defaults. No shared credentials, no PHI in logs, no PHI in error traces. The full posture lives at /security; the highlights sit below.

Encryption AES-256 at rest. TLS 1.2+ in transit. HSTS, CSP, SRI on every outbound request. All US regions
Audit logging Every send, reply, booking, export, role change, and auth event is logged with an append-only writer. Logs retained 7 years. Exportable any time
Access control Role-based with least-privilege defaults. SAML 2.0 SSO (Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace) on every tier. No shared logins. SSO included, not add-on
Incident response Written IR plan, quarterly-tested. Customer notified within 24 hours of any incident affecting their data. 24h breach SLA
PHI handling PHI scrubber enforced at the database query layer — no treatment details, procedure names, or diagnoses ever reach an outbound message body. Enforced in code
