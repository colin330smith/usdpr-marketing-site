How we build this — and what we won't.

Anonymization. No single practice's data is identifiable in any benchmark. We require n≥30 campaigns per segment before publishing a number. Outlier trimming at 5th–95th percentile before median computation.

No cross-tenant peek. usdpr. practices opt in individually to the benchmarks program. A practice that opts out has its data excluded from every aggregate — not just the public dashboard. Data commitments.
