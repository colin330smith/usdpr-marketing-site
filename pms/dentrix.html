FAQ

Is this HIPAA-compliant with Dentrix?

Yes. We sign a Business Associate Agreement before any patient data moves (via HelloSign, ~2 minutes). Every send, reply, and booking is logged in an append-only audit log. PHI never appears in message bodies — a scrubber enforces this at send time. See /security for the full posture.

What if I want to switch PMS later?

Your data is always exportable from /onboarding/export or our REST API. No export fee. No format lock-in.

How do I handle patients who already got a message from Dentrix's built-in reminders?

We only contact patients you flag as "active, no recent reach" in the export. If your Dentrix hygiene recall already contacted them in the last 14 days, exclude them from the export.
