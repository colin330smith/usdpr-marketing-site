Common questions from orthodontic practices

What makes reactivation different for orthodontic practices?

Orthodontic patients have cadences no generic recall tool respects: 6/12/18-month retainer checks, paused-treatment re-engagement, debond windows. A single "we miss you" SMS — what Weave and Solutionreach ship — is the wrong message. USDPR segments by treatment phase.

How many patients can a typical orthodontic practice expect to reactivate?

In our 2026.1 pilot cohort, ortho practices ran at 15.3% reactivation on a 6-touch sequence. For a 1,500-patient practice with 7.5% annual lapse, that's ~17 from lapsed list alone in 60 days, plus paused-treatment + debond-window recoveries. The 30-patient guarantee easily clears.

Does USDPR integrate with Ortho2, Dolphin, Cloud9 Ortho?

CSV import works with every ortho PMS. The column-mapping wizard auto-detects your PMS's export format. Open Dental has direct API integration. Most practices are live in under 90 seconds.

How does USDPR handle referring-GP coordination?

Shared-patient workflows send dual-party communications (patient + referring GP) with minimum-necessary PHI under 45 CFR §164.506(c)(2) Treatment exception. Built-in referral-thank-you reciprocation helps preserve your referral relationships.

Can USDPR handle paused-treatment patients differently from recall patients?

Yes. Paused-treatment gets a distinct 4-touch sequence: day 14 gentle re-engagement, day 30 barrier-removal, day 60 clinical-continuation-risk framing, day 90 practitioner warm-handoff if applicable. Completely separate from retainer-check cadences.
