Is this you?

An honest fit check before you click.

Pilot-fit \u00B7 say yes if

These describe your practice.

  • You run a US dental practice (solo or multi-location) and "lost patients" is a real line item you track.
  • You have at least 1,000 active patients (any PMS \u2014 Open Dental direct, or CSV from Dentrix/Eaglesoft/Curve/etc.).
  • You can commit to a 90-minute founder onboarding in the first 14 days (front desk included).
  • You value a tool built narrow and deep over a 20-feature platform where reactivation is a checkbox.
  • You're OK with the 10DLC SMS wait (email runs the whole time; SMS layers in around day 14\u201321).
  • You'd write an honest review on G2/Capterra at day 60 \u2014 good review or bad, we just want the truth.
Not-a-fit \u00B7 say no if

These describe your needs instead.

  • You need a fully managed agency writing each campaign by hand. usdpr. is software, not a marketing firm.
  • You want per-rebook pricing. Not legal in most states \u2014 we won\u2019t build it.
  • You need same-day SMS with brand new number before carrier approval. No one can do that legally; don't believe vendors who claim otherwise.
  • Your PMS is niche and won't accept a weekly CSV export. We're a no-fit for a week or two until your PMS is scaffolded.
  • You want a multi-year prepay discount. Month-to-month only \u2014 we'd rather earn the next month than lock you in.
  • You're a DSO > 50 locations \u2014 the pilot is optimized for 1\u201320 location groups; email Colin for a larger-scale conversation.
