Canon § 08 · An honest fit check

Read this before you click.

✓

This fits when…

  • You're an orthodontic practice with 400+ active-treatment patients.
  • You use Open Dental, Dentrix, Cloud 9, or Ortho2.
  • You have at least one TC or OM who can receive a weekly call list and act on it.
  • You can sign a HIPAA BAA and connect your PMS within 10 business days.
  • You're willing to run a holdout control group for the 60-day measurement window.
—

Not for you if…

  • You run a GP dental practice and want hygiene recall. We're ortho-only; see /for-dental.
  • You're looking for bulk SMS blasts with no attribution — we don't do that.
  • You want auto-booking without TC involvement — we surface calls, your team books.
  • Your active chart is below 350 and the monthly won't pencil.
  • You can't sign a BAA before PHI moves — non-negotiable.
