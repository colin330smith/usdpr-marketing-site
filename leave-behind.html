§ 01

The figure nobody reports

$487M of shown-patient value goes unrecovered annually across US ortho practices.

Every orthodontist averages 696 active-treatment patients, plus an observation file, plus a consult pipeline, plus a post-debond retention cohort. One missed prompt, any of them lapse. PubMed data shows a 23% attendance decline per 6-month adherence gap. At a typical $5,108–$9,221 case fee, even a 2% slippage compounds to six figures of forfeited visits a year.
