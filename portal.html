Patient portal

A portal that's actually useful.

Most dental patient portals are read-only and almost never logged into. usdpr.'s portal is action-first: book next visit, confirm reminders, update intake, settle a balance, message the front desk. One login, mobile-native, works for reactivated patients the same way it works for active ones.

Six things it gets right.

01
Book + reschedule
Real calendar availability from the PMS. Booking rules respect hygienist scheduling, provider rules, and procedure-type blocks.
02
Confirm with one tap
Reminder notifications deep-link into the portal confirm flow — one tap, state synced back to the PMS immediately.
03
Update intake
Patient can update insurance, contact, medical history between visits. Delta emailed to the front desk for approval before it syncs to the PMS.
04
Message the front desk
Secure messaging under the BAA. Messages flow into the same operator console as reactivation replies — unified inbox, unified SLA.
05
Pay balance
Stripe-backed patient payments with saved cards. Auto-reminder for overdue balances — tuned to the TCPA + state collections rules.
06
Passwordless login
Magic-link email + SMS OTP. No password resets. MFA on re-auth for anything sensitive (insurance, medical, payment).
Patient reactivation + portal together = patients stay active, self-serve reschedules, and the front desk stops being a human scheduling engine. Competitors sell these as separate $X/mo add-ons; usdpr. ships them together.
