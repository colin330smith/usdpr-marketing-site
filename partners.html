How attribution works

Customer referral flow

You share a link like usdpr.netlify.app/signup?tier=practice_pro&ref=YOURCODE. Stripe Checkout captures the referral code in metadata. When the referred practice launches their pilot, the credit lands on your next invoice automatically. You see the claim on your dashboard under /onboarding/referrals after signup.

Strategic partner flow

We agree on a partner code in writing. You hand it out to client practices. Monthly MRR attributed to your code accrues; we pay out via Stripe or ACH by the 15th of the following month. Reporting dashboard in the partner portal tracks which clients are active, which churned, what your YTD earnings are.

Rules + limits

  • No self-referrals. The referring client cannot be the same as the new-signup client.
  • Credit applies once per unique (new_client, code) pair.
  • Strategic partner payouts start after the new customer completes their 14-day trial.
  • If the new customer churns in month 1, the referral credit is rescinded. After month 2, it's yours.
  • Anti-kickback compliance: we never pay per-patient or per-appointment. Per-customer-subscription only. Keeps both you and us inside every state's safe harbor.
