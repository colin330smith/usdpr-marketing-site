Common questions about switching

Can I use USDPR alongside Weave without canceling?

Yes. USDPR is designed to run in parallel. Weave continues handling phones, reminders, reviews, payments. USDPR runs reactivation. The two don't conflict.

Will switching lose my patient data?

No. Your patient data lives in your PMS (Dentrix, Open Dental, Eaglesoft). USDPR reads from the PMS or a CSV. Weave's data — reminders, reviews, forms — is untouched.

What happens to my Weave contract?

That's your call, on your timeline. Most Weave contracts have 15-30 day cancellation windows; time your cancellation to Weave's renewal date, not to USDPR's schedule.

Do I need to tell Weave I'm running USDPR?

No. They're not competing for the same feature surface. You're simply adding a dedicated reactivation tool alongside your existing patient-comms suite.
