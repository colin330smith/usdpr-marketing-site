Common questions about switching from RevenueWell

RevenueWell already has reactivation. Why would I need USDPR?

Reactivation is one of 15 features in RW; it's the only feature in USDPR. Their single-SMS approach averages 1-3% reactivation on a lapsed list. Our 5-touch specialty sequence averages 10-15%. On a 2,000-patient practice, that's 3-6 vs. 30-50 reactivated patients per campaign.

My RevenueWell came through Patterson Dental. Can I still switch?

Yes. Keep the Patterson relationship for supplies and PMS. Add USDPR as a separate direct subscription on Stripe. No conflict, no coordination needed.

What about RW's CDT-code targeting and patient-balance segmentation?

Useful for routine recall. USDPR segments by clinical cadence (hygiene vs. perio vs. ortho retention vs. implant follow-up) instead. If you're GP-only, keep RW's segmentation; if you're a specialty practice, our cadence engine was built for you.

Will I lose my RevenueWell data if I switch?

No. Your patient data lives in your PMS. RW's campaign history stays in RW (exportable to CSV any time). USDPR reads from your PMS or a CSV — it does not depend on RW data at all.
