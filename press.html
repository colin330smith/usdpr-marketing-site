Pre-approved quotes

Quote anything below without clearance.

The average US dental practice loses about 9% of its active patients every year to broken appointments and missed recall. Across 130,000 private practices, that's a $36 billion annual revenue leak — bigger than the entire US orthodontic-device market.

Colin Smith · Founder, usdpr. · State of Reactivation 2026

Reactivation is not a message problem. It's a sequencing and segmentation problem. A single SMS reactivates 1–3% of lapsed patients. A five-touch sequence against the same list reactivates 8–15%. Same front desk. Same list. Four to six times the result.

Colin Smith · Founder, usdpr.

Per-orthodontic patient recovery fees are a felony under Florida §817.505 and similar statutes in at least thirty states. Any vendor structured on success-fee pricing is operating outside the law in most of the country. Flat SaaS is not a marketing choice; it's the only compliant model.

Colin Smith · Founder, usdpr.

I'm the one person accountable for this product. That's a feature, not a bug. If it breaks, my phone rings. If the product needs fixing, I'm the one fixing it that afternoon — not a three-week support queue.

Colin Smith · Founder, usdpr. · founder letter

The SaaS pricing games dental vendors play — annual lock-ins, dark-pattern cancellations, per-patient fees that trigger state statutes — those are going away. The next three years are about earning the month with a product that actually works.

Colin Smith · Founder, usdpr.

Office managers are the unsung heroes of orthodontic patient recovery. They've been making 180 recall calls a year per 2,000 patients between checkout and insurance verification. The least we can do is hand them a tool that doesn't also require learning a new dashboard.

Colin Smith · Founder, usdpr.

usdpr. (US Dental Patient Recovery) is a patient-patient recovery software for US orthodontic practices, founded in 2025 by Colin Smith and based in Orlando, Florida. The platform runs a five-touch email + SMS + phone sequence against lapsed-recall and broken-appointment patients under a HIPAA BAA, with flat SaaS pricing from $997/month and a free Ortho Recovery Audit. Compliance with HIPAA, TCPA 10DLC, CAN-SPAM, and state anti-kickback statutes (e.g., Florida §817.505) is enforced in code at the database and dispatcher layer. The company is bootstrapped and operates without outside capital. usdentalpatientrecovery.com
